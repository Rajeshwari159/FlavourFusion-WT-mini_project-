SANJEEV KAPOOR

Sanjeev Kapoor is one of India's most renowned chefs, known for popularizing Indian cuisine globally through his television shows, cookbooks, and restaurants. He is the host of the long-running cooking show "Khana Khazana," which has aired in over 120 countries, making him a household name.

MADHURA BACHAL

Madhura Bachal is a popular YouTuber and chef known for her channel "MadhurasRecipe Marathi," where she shares simple and authentic Maharashtrian recipes. She has a vast following among Marathi-speaking audiences and has made cooking accessible and enjoyable for many through her easy-to-follow tutorials.

VIKAS KHANNA

Vikas Khanna is an award-winning chef, restaurateur, cookbook author, and humanitarian known for his innovative approach to Indian cuisine. He has appeared on various television shows, including "MasterChef India," and has written several bestselling cookbooks, showcasing his culinary expertise and passion for Indian food.

PANKAJ BHADOURIA

Pankaj Bhadouria gained fame after winning the first season of "MasterChef India" and has since become a prominent figure in the Indian culinary scene. She is known for her creative approach to cooking and has authored several cookbooks, sharing her recipes and culinary expertise with audiences across the country.

RANBIR BRAR

Ranveer Brar is a celebrated Indian chef, restaurateur, and television personality known for his innovative approach to Indian cuisine. He has hosted several cooking shows, including "The Great Indian Rasoi" and "Raja Rasoi Aur Andaaz Anokha," where he explores the culinary traditions of India.

ATUL KOCHHAR

Atul Kochhar is an acclaimed Indian chef known for his innovative take on traditional Indian cuisine. He is the first Indian chef to receive a Michelin star for his restaurant "Tamarind" in London and has appeared on various television shows, including "MasterChef India."

KUNAL KAPUR

Kunal Kapur is a celebrated Indian chef, restaurateur, and television personality known for his expertise in contemporary Indian cuisine. He has hosted several cooking shows, including "MasterChef India" and "My Yellow Table," and has authored several cookbooks, sharing his recipes and culinary insights with a wide audience.

MANISH MEHROTRA

Manish Mehrotra is an acclaimed Indian chef known for his innovative take on traditional Indian cuisine. He is the executive chef of the iconic Indian restaurant "Indian Accent" and has received international acclaim for his culinary creations, which blend Indian flavors with global influences.

SHIPRA KHANNA

Shipra Khanna is an Indian chef and cookbook author known for winning the second season of "MasterChef India." She has authored several cookbooks, hosted cooking shows, and conducted culinary workshops, sharing her passion for cooking and creativity with audiences across the country.
